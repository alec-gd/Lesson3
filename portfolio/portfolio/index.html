Conference Branding Package Project

I created a brand guide for a client wanting to create a conference titled "Think Big Conference". The description of the conference is to bring business and sustainability togeher on a simple and communicative level. I used the natural themed background to add movement while not being to distracting from the rest of the logo. I used the text itself to contain the front of the logo. I also collected extra tag-lines, colors, fonts and a pattern to pair with the brand.
